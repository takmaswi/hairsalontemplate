Complete Hair Care Guide

Expert advice for every hair type and style preference

Natural Hair Care Essentials

🧴 Proper Washing Technique

  • • Pre-poo with oil 30 minutes before washing
  • • Use sulfate-free shampoo only on scalp
  • • Focus conditioner on mid-lengths and ends
  • • Rinse with cool water to seal cuticles
  • • Wash 1-2 times per week maximum

💧 The LOC Method

Leave-in conditioner - Apply to damp hair
Oil - Seal with natural oils like coconut or argan
Cream - Finish with a moisturizing cream

Apply this routine 2-3 times per week for optimal moisture retention.

🛡️ Protective Styling

  • • Braids, twists, or buns to minimize manipulation
  • • Satin/silk pillowcases or bonnets for sleep
  • • Avoid tight styles that cause tension
  • • Take breaks between protective styles
▶️

Natural Hair Care Tutorial

🌍 Zimbabwe Climate Tips

  • • Use heavier oils during dry season (May-October)
  • • Increase washing frequency during rainy season
  • • UV protection is crucial - use leave-in with SPF
  • • Drink extra water during hot months for internal hydration

Wig Care & Maintenance

✨ Daily Maintenance

  • • Gently brush from ends to roots
  • • Use wide-tooth comb for wet styling
  • • Store on mannequin head or wig stand
  • • Avoid sleeping in your wig
  • • Keep away from heat sources

🛁 Washing Your Wig

  1. 1. Detangle gently before washing
  2. 2. Use cold water and sulfate-free shampoo
  3. 3. Soak for 10-15 minutes, don't rub
  4. 4. Rinse thoroughly with cool water
  5. 5. Apply conditioner to mid-lengths and ends
  6. 6. Air dry on wig stand - never use heat

💇‍♀️ Styling Guidelines

  • • Use heat protectant before any heat styling
  • • Keep heat tools below 300°F (150°C)
  • • Trim split ends regularly
  • • Use wig-specific products for best results
👩‍🦱

Wig Styling Guide

⏰ Wig Lifespan Guide

Synthetic Wigs: 3-6 months with daily wear
Human Hair Wigs: 6-12 months with proper care
Glueless Wigs: 8-15 months (less manipulation)
Lace Front Wigs: 6-10 months depending on installation

Hair Extension Care

💦 Washing Routine

  • • Wash every 2-3 weeks (or as needed)
  • • Use lukewarm water to prevent tangling
  • • Apply shampoo from roots to ends gently
  • • Deep condition weekly for moisture
  • • Air dry when possible to prevent damage

🪄 Detangling Process

  1. 1. Apply leave-in conditioner or detangling spray
  2. 2. Start from the ends and work upward
  3. 3. Use a wide-tooth comb or paddle brush
  4. 4. Work in small sections to avoid breakage
  5. 5. Be extra gentle around attachment points

🌙 Nighttime Care

  • • Braid loosely or tie in a low ponytail
  • • Use a silk or satin pillowcase
  • • Apply a small amount of oil to ends
  • • Avoid tight hair ties that cause breakage
💇‍♀️

Extension Care Tutorial

🔧 Installation Tips

  • • Clip-ins: Remove daily to prevent tension
  • • Sew-ins: Professional installation recommended
  • • Tape-ins: Replace tape every 6-8 weeks
  • • Micro-links: Tighten every 6-8 weeks

Seasonal Hair Care for Zimbabwe

☀️ Dry Season (May - October)

  • • Increase deep conditioning to twice weekly
  • • Use heavier oils like castor or olive oil
  • • Protect from UV rays with leave-in SPF
  • • Stay hydrated - drink more water
  • • Use humidifier indoors if possible

🌧️ Rainy Season (November - April)

  • • Wash more frequently due to humidity
  • • Use lighter oils to prevent buildup
  • • Anti-frizz products become essential
  • • Protective styles work best in humidity
  • • Ensure hair is completely dry before styling

🌱 Year-Round Essentials

  • • Monthly protein treatments for strength
  • • Trim every 8-12 weeks for healthy ends
  • • Scalp massages to promote blood flow
  • • Regular exercise and balanced diet
  • • Consistent nighttime hair routine

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common hair care questions from our experts

For most natural hair types, washing 1-2 times per week is ideal. Over-washing can strip natural oils, leading to dryness. In Zimbabwe's climate, you may need to adjust frequency based on the season - wash more often during humid months and less during dry season.

High-quality human hair extensions can last 6-12 months with proper care. Clip-ins typically last longer since they're removed daily. Sew-ins should be reinstalled every 6-8 weeks. The lifespan depends on hair quality, maintenance routine, and styling frequency.

Yes, 100% human hair extensions can be colored, but we recommend professional coloring. Virgin hair takes color best. Always do a strand test first and use quality products. Note that bleaching can damage the hair and reduce its lifespan.

HD (High Definition) lace is thinner and more transparent than regular lace, creating a more natural hairline that virtually disappears when applied. It's perfect for those wanting the most realistic look, though it requires more careful handling due to its delicate nature.
